Top 10 TikTok Influencers

TikTok has rapidly become one of the most popular social media platforms, known for its short-form video content that entertains and inspires millions. This site showcases the top 10 TikTok influencers based on their follower counts, highlighting their unique content and impact on the platform.

The Rock (Dwayne Johnson)

Followers: 70M+

"The Rock" Dwayne Johnson is an American actor, producer, businessman, and former professional wrestler. He shares motivational content, fitness routines, and glimpses of his personal life on TikTok.

Burak Özdemir

Followers: 70M+

Burak Özdemir, also known as CZN Burak, is a Turkish chef who is famous for his cooking videos and his signature big smile. His TikTok content showcases giant meals and creative dishes.

Will Smith

Followers: 70M+

Will Smith is an American actor, rapper, and film producer. His TikTok account is filled with creative videos, comedy sketches, and behind-the-scenes content from his acting career.

Kimberly Loaiza

Followers: 80M+

Kimberly Loaiza is a Mexican singer and social media personality. She shares music videos, lifestyle content, and personal moments on her TikTok channel.

Zach King

Followers: 80M+

Zach King is an American internet personality, filmmaker, and illusionist known for his "magic vines" – short videos digitally edited to look as if he is doing magic.

Addison Rae

Followers: 80M+

Addison Rae is an American social media personality and dancer. She is known for her dance videos on TikTok and has also ventured into music and acting.

Bella Poarch

Followers: 90M+

Bella Poarch is a Filipino-American singer and social media personality. She gained fame for her lip-sync videos, particularly the "M to the B" video, which became the most-liked video on TikTok.

Mr. Beast (Jimmy Donaldson)

Followers: 100M+

Mr. Beast, also known as Jimmy Donaldson, is an American YouTuber and philanthropist. His TikTok content includes crazy challenges, philanthropy, and behind-the-scenes footage of his YouTube videos.

Charli D'Amelio

Followers: 150M+

Charli D'Amelio is an American social media personality and dancer. She is known for her dance videos on TikTok and was the first person to earn both 50 million and 100 million followers on the platform.

Khaby Lame

Followers: 160M+

Khaby Lame is an Italian social media personality known for his silent TikTok videos where he humorously points out overly complicated life hacks.

Note: The follower counts mentioned above are approximate and may change over time as new followers are gained or lost.
